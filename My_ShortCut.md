## vim
|Command    |Keybinding            |
|-----------|----------------------|
|:wq        |write and quit        |
|:q!        |force quit            |
|w          |next word             |
|b          |previous word         |
|0          |beginning of the line |
|$          |end of the line       |
|gg         |first line of the file|
|G          |last line of the file |
|Ctrl+f     |page down             |
|Ctrl+b     |page up               |
|50G        |line number 50        |
